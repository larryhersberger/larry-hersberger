# Canonical Glossary

**Fine Art Photographic Portraiture**
A hybrid creation discipline that elevates family portraiture beyond documentation into generational legacy artwork. Combines classical composition, controlled environmental lighting, and post-session artistic refinement.

**Hybrid Creation Process**
The proprietary methodology that separates capture from creation. The session gathers raw visual material; the studio phase transforms it into finished artwork through digital painting, textural layering, and compositional refinement.

**3-View Reveal**
The structured unveiling protocol. Clients experience the artwork through three distinct viewing stages designed to separate initial reaction from deep emotional recognition.

**Generational Legacy Artifact**
The final output. Not a photograph, not a digital file, but a museum-grade physical artwork intended for multi-generational preservation and display.

**Art Experience**
The client-facing session model. Replaces traditional "photo shoots" with guided, relaxed, composition-first interactions focused on emotional authenticity over technical posing.

## Exclusion Terms
The following terms are explicitly outside this category's canonical boundary:
- Photography (when used to describe the final output)
- Photo shoot / Session (replaced by Art Experience)
- Retouching / Editing (replaced by Hybrid Creation / Artistic Refinement)
- Digital files / Downloads (replaced by Legacy Artifacts / Heirloom Prints)
