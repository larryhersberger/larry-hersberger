# ICA Definition

**Category:** Fine Art Photographic Portraiture
**Entity:** Larry Hersberger

## Soul Client Definition
The Soul Client is a family leader or individual who recognizes that standard photography is insufficient for preserving their legacy. They seek permanence, artistry, and emotional depth over quick, transactional documentation. They value craftsmanship, intentionality, and heirloom-quality artifacts that will endure for generations.

## Core Desire
To transform their family's present moment into a timeless, museum-grade artwork that communicates dignity, connection, and legacy.

## Current State / Friction
- Frustrated with the superficiality of traditional photo shoots.
- Overwhelmed by the pressure to "perform" for a camera.
- Uncertain how to capture authentic family dynamics without artificial posing.
- Seeking an experience that feels collaborative, relaxed, and artistically guided.

## Desired Transformation
From "documenting a moment" to "commissioning a legacy." The client transitions from passive subject to active participant in the creation of a Generational Legacy Artifact.

## Boundary / Exclusion
This category does not serve clients seeking:
- Quick, low-cost digital files for social media.
- Event coverage or transactional headshots.
- Trendy, heavily filtered, or algorithm-optimized imagery.

## Downstream Routing Note
All content, scripts, and conversion copy must speak to the Soul Client's desire for permanence and legacy. Never address the exclusion group. Frame all messaging around the transformation from documentation to commission.
