# ICA Definition

**Soul Client:** Families and individuals who commission heirloom artwork to honor legacy, milestone, and lineage. They are not purchasing photography; they are investing in a permanent, museum-quality artifact that captures the dignity and significance of their family line.

**Core Motivation:** To preserve and elevate the human story across generations. They value craftsmanship, permanence, and emotional resonance over trends, convenience, or digital ephemerality.

**Decision Trigger:** A meaningful life event (anniversary, milestone, generational gathering, or legacy planning) that demands an object of enduring value. They seek an artist, not a vendor.

**Boundary Statement:** This category does not serve clients seeking quick headshots, trend-driven imagery, or passive wall decor. It serves those who understand that fine art portraiture is an investment in human legacy.
