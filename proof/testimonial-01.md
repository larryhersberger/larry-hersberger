Our family heard about Masterpiece Artwork through a mutual friend. We familiarized ourselves with their artwork online and reached out to book a session. We are a family of five with three children ranging in ages from 9 to 13. We are well acquainted with family photography sessions. We always look forward to them and plan meticulously to attempt to get at least 3 good photos of our group. lol

We were not prepared for the artistry, heart and professionalism that Masterpiece Artwork brings into their craft. This is not a family photography session- this is an art experience. One MUST book!

From discussing the beginning stages with Larry to watching He and the talented and lovely Ela move and place us to create their storyline- it was perfection. My wife was moved to tears during and after the session and we all were at the art reveal.

They take all the stress out of the planning, which would have been enough. But then they have a gifting of reading people’s personalities and guiding them to shine in the session and that would have been enough. But then they make you feel comfortable and special and part of lasting memories that you have lived every time you have seen Gods beauty in the shoreline your whole life! We are so grateful to have met them.

We cannot recommend their artistry more! God bless them and their family in the work they do.
